cloudytowers. is curated by A. M. Hemingway, an American poet and artist based in Columbus, Ohio. This presentation was created to search for images of a specific visual tone. This is a space for mediums to dialogue about memory’s relationship with time, self-reflection, and how these relate to meaning. Hemingway’s passion for unique problem solving and data analysis is used as a means for storytelling, both in analog and digital mediums.
